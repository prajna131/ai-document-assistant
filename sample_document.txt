ZentaraFlow Smart Water Bottle - Product Guide

Overview
ZentaraFlow is a smart water bottle developed by Nuvexa Labs, first released on
March 14, 2024. It helps users stay hydrated by tracking water intake and
reminding them to drink throughout the day.

Key Specifications
- Capacity: 750 milliliters
- Battery life: 21 days on a single charge
- Weight: 340 grams
- Material: Medical-grade stainless steel with a bamboo cap
- Colors available: Midnight Blue, Coral Pink, and Forest Green
- Water temperature sensor accuracy: plus or minus 0.5 degrees Celsius

Smart Features
The bottle contains a sensor called the HydroCore chip, which measures how much
water is consumed. It syncs with the ZentaraFlow mobile app over Bluetooth 5.2.
The app calculates a daily hydration goal based on the user's weight, activity
level, and the local weather temperature.

The glowing ring on the bottle cap lights up blue when it is time to drink. If
the user ignores three reminders in a row, the ring switches to a pulsing red.

Pricing and Warranty
ZentaraFlow is priced at 49 US dollars. It comes with a 2-year limited warranty.
Replacement bamboo caps can be purchased separately for 8 US dollars each.

Customer Support
For support, customers can email help@nuvexalabs.example or call the support line,
which is open Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Pacific Time. The average response
time for email support is under 4 hours.

Fun Fact
The name "ZentaraFlow" comes from combining the words "Zen" (calm) and "Tara"
(a star), reflecting the company's goal of making hydration effortless and calm.
